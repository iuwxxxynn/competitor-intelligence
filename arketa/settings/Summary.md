# Settings - Arketa

**URL:** https://dashboard.arketa.com/dashboard/settings
**Page Type:** Settings / Configuration
**Crawl Date:** 2026-05-08

## Purpose
Central configuration hub for all studio settings. Controls business profile, booking rules, payments, notifications, integrations, and branding.

## Settings Categories (9 total)
- Business Profile: Studio name, address, timezone, contact info
- Booking Settings: Cancellation windows, waitlist rules, booking lead times
- Payments: Payment processors, currency, tax settings
- Notifications: Email and SMS templates for confirmations and reminders
- Integrations: Third-party tool connections (Zapier, Mailchimp, etc.)
- Custom Fields: Client and booking custom data fields
- Branded Mobile App: Mobile app configuration (premium)
- Website Widget: Embed configuration and styling
- Staff and Roles: Permissions and access (links to manage/team)

## CTAs and Buttons
- Save Changes: Present on most sub-setting pages
- Connect (Integrations): Initiates OAuth or API key entry for third-party services
- Upgrade prompts: Appear on premium features like Branded Mobile App

## UX Observations
- Settings are organized into a clear category list on the left with content on the right
- Each category expands into multiple sub-settings
- Breadcrumb navigation helps track location within nested settings
- Premium features show upgrade CTAs inline rather than hiding them

## Business Observations
- 9 distinct settings categories suggests mature product with broad configuration surface
- Inline upsell for Branded Mobile App within settings is strategic (high conversion context)
