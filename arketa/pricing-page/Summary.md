# Pricing Page - Arketa

**URL:** https://arketa.com/pricing
**Page Type:** Public Marketing / Pricing
**Crawl Date:** 2026-05-08

## Purpose
Present pricing plans for both studio operators and individual instructors. Primary conversion page for new signups.

## Plan Structure

### Studio Plans
- Core: Entry-level plan for new studios
- Growth: Mid-tier with expanded features
- Suite: Full-featured plan for established studios

### Individual Instructor Plans
- Basic: Starting at $49/mo for solo instructors
- Growth: Mid-tier individual plan
- Suite: Full-featured individual plan

## Key Features by Plan (observed)
- Core: Basic booking, scheduling, client management
- Growth: Adds marketing tools, automations, advanced analytics
- Suite: Full CRM, mobile app, multi-location, all integrations

## CTAs and Buttons
- Get Started / Start Free Trial: Primary CTA per plan
- Compare Plans: Toggle or link to feature comparison table
- Segment selector: Studio vs Individual toggle at top
- Contact Sales: Appears for enterprise/multi-location inquiries

## UX Observations
- Dual-track pricing (Studio vs Individual) requires segment selection upfront
- 3-tier pricing per segment follows standard SaaS best practices
- Individual plan starting price ($49/mo) is clearly visible and low-friction anchor
- Feature comparison likely uses checkmarks per row for scanability

## Business Observations
- Separate studio/individual tracks signals different buyer personas and sales motions
- $49/mo individual entry point is competitive for solo instructor market
- Suite tier likely bundles premium add-ons (Branded App, advanced marketing) to drive upsell
- Lack of public pricing for multi-location/enterprise suggests sales-led motion

## Operational Observations
- Pricing page is the primary self-serve acquisition funnel entry point
- Clear tier differentiation reduces support burden from confused prospects
- Business type segmentation on pricing mirrors BUSINESS TYPES nav dropdown
