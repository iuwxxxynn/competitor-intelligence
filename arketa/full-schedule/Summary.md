# Arketa - Full Schedule

**URL:** https://dashboard.arketa.com/dashboard/classes/full_schedule
**Captured:** 2026-05-08

## Purpose
Master calendar view for all scheduled classes, appointments, and events.

## Tabs
Full Schedule | Classes | Appointments | Events | + Custom View

## Filters
- This Week (with prev/next arrows)
- All instructors
- All locations
- All rooms
- All classes
- All categories
- Display options

## Primary CTAs
- **Add new** dropdown: New class, New appointment, New semi-private, New event

## UX Observations
- Empty state with clear CTA: 'No classes scheduled. Your schedule is wide open.'
- Help link to 'Creating & Managing Classes'
- Custom View tab for saved filter configurations

## Business Observations
- 4 service types in one calendar view
- Multi-room and multi-location filtering
