# Staff / Team - Arketa

**URL:** https://dashboard.arketa.com/dashboard/manage/team
**Page Type:** Staff Management
**Crawl Date:** 2026-05-08

## Purpose
Manage all staff members, pay rates, roles, and permissions. Central hub for workforce administration.

## Tabs
- Team: List of all staff with name, role, and status
- Pay Rates: Individual pay rate assignments per staff member
- Pay Rate Templates: Reusable pay rate configurations
- Reporting: Staff-specific payroll summaries
- Roles and Permissions: Define custom roles with granular access controls

## CTAs and Buttons
- Add Staff Member: Opens modal to invite or create new staff
- Roles and Permissions tab: Manage what each role can see and do
- Pay Rate Templates: Create templates for faster rate assignment

## UX Observations
- Tab-based layout organizes workforce management across 5 distinct functions
- Pay rates and templates are separated allowing standardization across staff types
- Roles and Permissions suggests fine-grained access control is a first-class feature
- No bulk-action controls on staff list; operations appear per-member

## Business Observations
- Separate pay rate templates indicate multi-staff studios are a core use case
- Reporting inside Staff section implies payroll reporting is distinct from business analytics
- Roles and Permissions depth signals enterprise and multi-location readiness

## Operational Observations
- Competitors with simpler staff management will lose to Arketa on admin depth
- Pay rate templates reduce setup time for growing studios
- Permission granularity is a key differentiator vs lightweight scheduling tools
